Sugar Cookie Recipe!

A fun sugar cookie recipe that's sure to make all your friends come back for more!

Ingredients

  • 2.75 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (or honey)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 375 F.
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a small bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in flower mixture.
  6. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Let cool and enjoy!
