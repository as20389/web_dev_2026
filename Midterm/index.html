Bio

I am a student who is interested in creativity, communication, and technology. I like exploring design ideas, building beginner-friendly coding projects, and finding ways to make my work feel welcoming and visually interesting.

This portfolio is a collection of my experiences, class projects, and personal growth. I wanted it to feel soft, playful, and polished while still being simple enough for a class website.

A few things that represent me are learning, organization, design, collaboration, and trying new things even when I am still a beginner.

Love to learn, learn to love.
