# Sugar Cookies!

# This is a family friendly cookie dish that you can bring to parties, movies, and games! It's a quick recipe that everybody is sure to love!

## Ingredients!
- 2.75 cups **all-purpose flour**
- 1 teaspoon **baking soda**
- 0.5 teaspoon **baking powder**
- 1 cup **butter, softened**
- 1.5 cups **white sugar**
- 1 **large egg**
- 1 teaspoon **vanilla extract**


## Instructions!
- Gather all ingredients! Preheat the oven to *375 degrees Fahrenheit*.
- Stir flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a *small bowl*.
- Beat sugar and butter together in a *large bowl with an electric mixer* until smooth.
- Beat in *egg and vanilla*.
- *Gradually blend* in flour mixture.
- Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and *place two inches apart* onto ungreased baking sheets.
- Bake in the preheated oven *until edges are just golden*, 8-10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before moving to a wire rack to cool completely.
- Enjoy!!
